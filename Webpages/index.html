Is it a Nice Day for a Bike Ride?


Bike-Sharing has become increasingly popular in major cities. Here in the Twin Cities, we have the bike-sharing nonprofit Nice Ride MN. Customers can rent bikes at stations scattered throughout the city. Each station has docks for several bikes.


The project looks to answer the following questions:

  • What is the relationship between weather and bike riding activity?
  • How can we predict the demand at each station?
  • Should bikes be reallocated from low-demand stations to high demand stations?

In addition to the SKlearn regression models, tableau and matplot-lib were used to create visualizations to describe the data.
